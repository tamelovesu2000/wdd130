Nigeria

Nigeria, a country located in West Africa, is a melting pot of diverse cultures, traditions, and customs. With over 250 ethnic groups, each with its unique language, beliefs, and practices, Nigerian culture is a rich and complex tapestry. One of the defining characteristics of Nigerian culture is hospitality. Nigerians are known for their warm welcomes and respect for guests. Visitors are often treated with lavish feasts, reflecting the importance of community and social relationships in Nigerian culture.
